Our Belief

Wine has always rewarded those who drink it together.

"Hong Kong has one of the world's most sophisticated wine cultures — and no app that does it justice. Until now."

Zero wine duty since 2008. Asia's highest per-capita consumption. A city of drinkers who take wine seriously without taking themselves too seriously — and a growing underground of natural wine enthusiasts who've been borrowing Instagram to share their pours.

Rumble Vines is the platform they've been missing. Not a global label scanner. Not a fine wine marketplace. A genuinely local, bilingual, community-first app where real-world events and digital discovery reinforce each other.

We're starting in Hong Kong. The world follows.

The App

Five features.
One community.

Every feature exists because wine is better when shared — and the best discoveries happen when digital and real-world meet.

01 — SCAN & LOG
Tasting Journal
Scan any label instantly. AI-guided tasting note prompts help anyone describe what they're drinking — from curious beginner to seasoned collector. Share your pour with your community.
Core Feature
02 — SOCIAL FEED
Community Feed
A living feed of what friends and followed accounts are drinking right now. Like, comment, save. Follow our founding sommelier for weekly natural wine picks.
Social-First
03 — EVENTS
Events & Tastings
Browse and book curated wine nights, blind tastings, and producer dinners — all in-app. The only wine platform that makes real-world social experiences bookable in seconds.
Our Moat
04 — CELLAR
Cellar & Collection
Track your bottles, share your cellar with a partner, and get notified when a wine hits its drinking window. Shared access for households from day one — something no major competitor offers.
Shared Access
05 — CHALLENGE
Blind Tasting Challenges
Virtual and in-person blind tasting rounds within your friend groups. No competitor has this mechanic. Knowledge becomes social currency.
Zero Competition
